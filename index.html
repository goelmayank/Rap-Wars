Welcome

RAP WARS is the only nationwide freestyle rap battling competition previously featuring Smokey and Brodha V as judges and also include artists like Rob C and Cardoz Lester as the earlier winners. An audience of more than 1000 people provides a brilliant platform for young rappers to showcase their abilities and develop a greater fan following.
