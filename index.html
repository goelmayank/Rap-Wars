Welcome

RAP WARS is an initiative to provide a platform for underground rap battlers in India. Today, it is the only nationwide freestyle rap battling competitions in India. Rap Wars is also one of the kernel events at Oasis, the second largest cultural fest in the country.

After five successful editions, Rap Wars now brings the battle to your own city.  Rap Wars shall now be conducting it's elimination rounds in Delhi, Bangalore, Mumbai and Kolkata.

Date: 19th of October to the 24th of October, 2016
