About

What is Rap Wars?

Rap Wars was started as an initiative to provide an oppurtunity to a underground Rap battlers in India. Today Rap Wars is the only nationwide freestyle rap battling competition in India. Rap Wars is also one of the kernel Prelims at Oasis,the second largest cultural fest in Asia. After five successful editions, Rap Wars now brings the battle to your own city. Rap Wars shall now be conducting it's elimination rounds in Delhi, Bangalore, Mumbai and Kolkata.

Past Judges and Winners

Rap Wars has previously featured judges like Smokey, Brodha V, Vivian Divine and Xstacy Sash. We also had participants such as Rob C, Kav-E, Akash Aeke Khandelwal and Dhruv Singh as previous winners some of whom have gone on to score major record deals.
