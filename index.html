About Us

What is Rap Wars?

Rap Wars was started as an initiative to provide an opportunity to underground Rap battlers in India. Today, Rap Wars is the largest nationwide rap battling competition in India. Rap Wars is also one of the kernel events at Oasis, the second largest cultural fest in Asia. After five successful editions, Rap Wars now brings the battle to your own city.
